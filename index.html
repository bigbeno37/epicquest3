The #1 Questing Plugin for Bukkit Servers

EpicQuest is an easy to use yet highly customizable quest plugin. Immerse your players into Minecraft with a commandless questing experience through villagers, bounty signs and a book to show your progress!

Unrestricted Gameplay

With EpicQuest, you can have as many tasks as you want, whenever you want! You can have multiple tasks per quest as well as multiple quests at the exact same time

Unlimited Potential

Featuring many different Quest Types and Quest Rewards (such as Money, Permissions, and even Command Execution), YOU are in control of user experience. Personalise each and every quest to your heart's content, and focus on writing adventures instead of worrying about code

Seamless Integration

Containing native integration with Vault, Citizens, Heroes and MythicMobs, EpicQuest makes it easy to integrate into active servers as well as configuring Quest Rewards

Open-source License

EpicQuest is licensed under the open-source MIT license. This means that you can use this plugin in any way you want to, so long as you include the MIT license with it
